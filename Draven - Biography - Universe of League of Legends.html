Jasper Draven
the Kraken Slayer King

Jasper Draven was a prominent League of Legends player, known for his innovative strategies and exceptional gameplay. With a deep understanding of the game mechanics and a penchant for pushing the boundaries, he quickly rose through the ranks and gained notoriety in the gaming community.

In one of his matches, Jasper came across an item known as Kraken Slayer. Intrigued by its unique properties and potential synergies, he decided to experiment with it and incorporate it into his gameplay. Kraken Slayer is an attack damage-based Mythic item that provides bonus attack speed, critical strike chance, and a powerful passive effect called "Bring It Down!" This passive allows the user's basic attacks to deal bonus true damage on every third hit, making it a strong choice for champions who rely on auto-attacks to deal damage.

Jasper Draven, a gifted ADC player, immediately saw the potential of Kraken Slayer on various marksman champions. He began building it on his signature champion, Draven, and quickly discovered its immense power in shredding through enemy tanks and squishy targets alike. As he continued to utilize this item in his games, he consistently outperformed other ADC players, earning himself a reputation as the "Kraken Slayer King."

Soon, word of Jasper's innovative Kraken Slayer build spread throughout the League of Legends community. Players started to follow his lead, experimenting with the item on their favorite champions, and quickly realized its potential in helping them secure victories. As a result, Kraken Slayer became a popular and sought-after item in the game, with many players attributing its rise to prominence to Jasper Draven himself.

Jasper's unique approach to the game, combined with his undeniable skill, cemented his status as a trendsetter within the League of Legends community. Through his pioneering efforts, he brought Kraken Slayer to the forefront of the meta, forever changing the landscape of itemization in the game.
